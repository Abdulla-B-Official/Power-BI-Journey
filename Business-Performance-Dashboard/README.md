#  Business Performance Dashboard 

An interactive **Power BI Dashboard** designed to analyze **business performance, KPIs, revenue trends, and operational insights** using business intelligence and data visualization techniques.

##  Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze and monitor:

* Business Performance Metrics
* KPI Tracking & Analysis
* Revenue & Profit Trends
* Operational Performance
* Data-Driven Decision Support
* Business Insights & Reporting

This dashboard helps organizations make **data-driven decisions** by transforming business data into actionable insights.

##  Key KPIs

* **Total Revenue**
* **Total Profit**
* **Business Growth**
* **Performance Metrics**
* **Operational Efficiency**
* **KPI Tracking**

### Business Questions

* How is business performance changing over time?
* What are the key revenue and profit trends?
* Which KPIs contribute most to business growth?
* How can operational performance be improved?
* Which business areas require attention?

##  Project Process

1. **Data Collection** – Imported business datasets into Power BI
2. **Data Cleaning** – Processed and transformed data using Power Query
3. **Data Modeling** – Built relationships between datasets
4. **KPI Creation** – Developed DAX measures for business analysis
5. **Dashboard Development** – Created interactive reports and visualizations
